<p><ul><li>Positive Pressure Isolator</li><li>Negative Pressure Isolator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rat isolator market encompasses two primary types: positive pressure isolators and negative pressure isolators. Positive pressure isolators maintain a higher internal pressure than the external environment, preventing contamination while allowing for a controlled atmosphere suitable for specific experiments. In contrast, negative pressure isolators create a lower internal pressure, effectively containing hazardous materials and protecting the external environment. Both types are essential in research and pharmaceutical laboratories to ensure safety and integrity when handling sensitive biological materials.</p></p>
