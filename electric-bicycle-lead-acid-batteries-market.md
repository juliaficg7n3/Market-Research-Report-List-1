<p><p>The electric bicycle lead-acid batteries market primarily features two types: VRLA (Valve-Regulated Lead-Acid) batteries and flooded batteries. VRLA batteries are sealed, maintenance-free, and ideal for applications requiring efficient energy storage without spillage, making them suitable for e-bikes. Flooded batteries, while less common in e-bikes, are often used in higher-capacity applications due to their lower cost and ability to provide high discharge rates. Both types cater to different consumer needs, balancing performance, cost, and maintenance requirements.</p></p>
