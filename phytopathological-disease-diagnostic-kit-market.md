<p><p>The Phytopathological Disease Diagnostic Kit market offers two primary types of tests: serological tests and molecular tests. Serological tests detect plant pathogens through antibodies, providing quick and reliable results, often useful for routine screenings. In contrast, molecular tests identify specific DNA or RNA sequences of pathogens, offering higher sensitivity and specificity. Both testing methods are essential for effective disease management in agriculture, enabling farmers and researchers to diagnose plant diseases accurately and implement timely control measures.</p></p>
