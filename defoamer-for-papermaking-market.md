<p><p>In the papermaking industry, defoamers are essential additives that serve two main purposes: eliminating bubbles and suppressing their formation. Defoamers designed to eliminate bubbles effectively break down existing foam in the pulp and papermaking process, enhancing product quality and reducing defects. Conversely, suppressing bubbles involves preventing foam from forming in the first place, thus ensuring smoother operations during manufacturing. Both types are crucial for maintaining the efficiency of production processes and achieving optimal paper quality.</p></p>
