<p><p>Taper fused fiber bundles are specialized optical components used primarily in fiber optics for combining or distributing light signals. The market can be segmented into two main types: power combiners and pump combiners. Power combiners are designed to merge multiple light sources into a single, more powerful output, enhancing efficiency in applications like lasers. Conversely, pump combiners focus on combining pump light for fiber amplifiers, optimizing signal amplification in telecommunications and industrial setups. Both types cater to diverse industries, including telecommunications, medical devices, and sensors.</p></p>
