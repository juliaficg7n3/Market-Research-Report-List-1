<p><strong>The Phase Contrast Microscopy Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Traditional Phase-Contrast Microscopy</li><li>Quantitative Phase-Contrast Microscopy</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The phase contrast microscopy market is primarily divided into two types: traditional phase-contrast microscopy and quantitative phase-contrast microscopy. Traditional phase-contrast microscopy enhances the visibility of transparent specimens by converting phase shifts in light into variations in brightness, making it suitable for biological and medical samples. On the other hand, quantitative phase-contrast microscopy provides detailed quantitative data regarding specimen thickness and refractive index, allowing for advanced analysis of live cells and tissues, enhancing research capabilities in biology and material science.</p></p>
