<p><p>The water-soluble fertilizers market includes four primary types: nitrogenous, phosphatic, potassic, and micronutrients. Nitrogenous fertilizers enhance plant growth by providing essential nitrogen, while phosphatic fertilizers promote root development and flowering through phosphorus supply. Potassic fertilizers improve overall plant health and resistance to stress by supplying potassium. Micronutrients, though required in smaller amounts, play crucial roles in various physiological processes, ensuring optimal plant growth and productivity. Together, these fertilizers cater to diverse agricultural needs, enhancing crop yield and quality.</p></p>
