<p><p>Gelatin type hard capsules are categorized into Type I and Type II based on their composition and manufacturing processes. Type I capsules are made from a blend of gelatin and water, often containing no preservatives, and are ideal for moisture-sensitive formulations. Type II capsules, on the other hand, are produced using gelatin and include preservatives, offering better stability and shelf life. Both types are widely used in pharmaceuticals and dietary supplements, catering to varying formulation needs and consumer preferences.</p></p>
