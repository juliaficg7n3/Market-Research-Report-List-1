<p><ul><li>Optical zoom</li><li>Extended optical zoom</li><li>Digital zoom</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Zoom Camera Objectives Market includes three main types: Optical Zoom, Extended Optical Zoom, and Digital Zoom. Optical Zoom utilizes physical lens adjustments to magnify images without quality loss, making it preferred for professional photography. Extended Optical Zoom enhances traditional optical zoom ranges, capturing distant subjects with better clarity. Digital Zoom, on the other hand, crops the image, often leading to a decrease in quality. Each type caters to different user needs, impacting device specifications and market demand.</p></p>
