<p><p>The turbofan engine market comprises three main types: low-bypass, afterburning, and high-bypass turbofans. Low-bypass turbofans, commonly used in military aircraft, prioritize speed and thrust. Afterburning turbofans, also favored in military applications, enhance thrust through afterburning, allowing for faster speeds. High-bypass turbofans are predominantly used in commercial aviation due to their efficiency and quieter operation, making them ideal for passenger jets. Each type serves distinct applications, balancing performance, efficiency, and operational requirements.</p></p>
