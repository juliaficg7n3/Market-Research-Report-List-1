<p><p>The nanoimprint lithography consumables market comprises three primary segments: nano imprint resin, resist, and templates. Nano imprint resins are specialized materials that provide the necessary properties for imprinting patterns onto substrates. Resists are light-sensitive materials used in photolithography processes to form patterns. Templates are precise molds that define the nanostructures transferred onto the resin during processing. Together, these consumables are essential for achieving high-resolution patterns and are critical in applications like semiconductors and nanotechnology.</p></p>
